Hi, I'm Jan Radomiński-Lasek

I am currently in last year of High School in Kraków, Poland, and recently I have been admitted to The Bachelor of Science Program at the École Polytechnique de Paris, France. The Program ends with a double major in mathematics and computer science and minor courses in physics, economics, political sciences and chemistry. The 450 students participating in the Program come from 37 different countries, creating a diverse and close-knit community. Unfortunately, studying at the École entails great expense. Nevertheless, I have signed a binding agreement with the School. The continuation of my studies in France will be demanding, but I must seize this opportunity.

A bit about me

I am in final year of Cracow's 5th High School, in a class with a mathematical-algorithmic profile, and I possess a profound interest in mathematics and algorithmics. Through my participation in various mathematical olympiads and workshops, I developed and gained several achievements. I am particularly drawn to the logical and systematic nature of mathematics, and I find immense satisfaction in solving complex problems.

My approach to problem-solving involves a proclivity towards unconventional solutions, which is why I value multidisciplinary education. I believe that by combining different areas of knowledge, I can develop novel solutions to intricate problems. I cherish innovation and am committed to lifelong learning. 

Additionally, I highly value the importance of building and sustaining diverse communities. I believe that bringing together people from different backgrounds and perspectives can foster innovation, creativity, and empathy. Throughout my academic and extracurricular activities, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with individuals from diverse backgrounds, and I have found that such experiences have been enriching and valuable. I am committed to promoting inclusive and collaborative environments where everyone's voice is heard and valued, and where we can work together towards common goals.

Hobbies and activities

In parallel to my interest in mathematics and algorithmics, I have many other passions. First of all, I have been training alpine skiing for a long time, achieving, among others, the following results:

• 30th place in the general classification of the youth cup of   Poland   2019
• 12th place in the XXV youth cup of Poland in alpine   skiing


I continue my passion by teaching others in Kraków and Zakopane ski clubs. Apart from skiing, I enjoy volleyball, climbing and sailing. I also love travelling, especially hitchhiking, and learning about different customs and viewpoints. In my free time I indulge in my passion for theatre, watching plays on Krakow's stages.



It is very important to me to help others, so I am socially involved in various ways. First and foremost, I am a member of the "Krzysiek Pomaga Pomagać" association, where I am responsible for coordinating the organisation of charity ski competitions year after year. I also get involved in charity events as a member of the student council at my high school.
